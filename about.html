Who We Are?

Stanford Legal is a leading law firm that comprises lawyers with specialized knowledge and expertise in various areas of Nigerian and International law. We guide our clients through difficult issues, helping them make the right decisions while using our insight and judgment in each situation.

We provide sound legal services and counsel for dispute resolution, cooperate services, commercial litigation, competition and regulatory practice, and business sectors. With delivery of top-notch services, we take pride in our superior advocacy, excellent work product, and personalized service.

More importantly, by thinking on behalf of our clients we anticipate what they want, provide what they need and build lasting relationships all the way. We essentially help our clients achieve their goals.

We are proudly Nigerian with a global reach, and our clients have emerged from different fields of professional endeavours. We have our offices located in two major cities in Nigeria – Lagos and Abuja – where we are able to continuously serve our clients.



OUR PEOPLE

Our team of experienced lawyers are specialized in different fields of law to deliver more value to clients. Indeed, we pride ourselves with having the best hands specialized fields – our competitive edge.
